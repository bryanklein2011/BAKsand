About BAK sand
Youth Beach Volleyball

The purpose of the BAK Beach Volleyball program is provide an environment where young women and men can reach their academic, athletic, and personal potential, both at BAK sand Volleyball and after high school. We believe that through the values of the program, student athletes will develop the life skills to be successful.

In the volleyball program athletes learn what it means to have Wolf Pride and how important it is to compete with passion. They are taught mental toughness and how to cope with adversity during matches, practices, as well as in the classroom and off the court. When an athlete leaves the volleyball program, they should have developed a sense of personal responsibility and an understanding of the hard work that is necessary for one to be successful in life.

Our role as coaches is to guide student athletes through the development of these values by developing relationships with our student athletes that allow for trust and respect. We must be patient, supportive, and listen but also be clear and honest with our expectations and decisions. We also expect to perform well in matches and push the student athlete out of their comfort zones to achieve success. We know the hard work that is necessary to have a successful program, and we are dedicated to pushing our athletes to those limits.

Competitive Regional

Our Competitive Regional group is designed for those athletes who have outgrown our Club group but are not yet ready to test their game at the national level. Many athletes in this group have aspirations of playing for their high school teams and want the skills to be able to do so. These athletes are on track to eventually join our Competitive Travel group should they choose to do so, but are not required to do so. Our Competitive Local group participates in local doubles tournaments to learn the ins and outs of competing at a high level but does not travel to out of state events, club vs club events, and national events. Our alternates for Club be Club events will be selected from this group ONLY if our National team players are unable to make it to an event. Basic strategies, game flow, and specific communication are solidified in this group as the core of great volleyball is built. In this program, you have the option to commit to the entire season at a discounted price and with other perks, or attend month to month.
