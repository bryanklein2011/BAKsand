Programs

Young athletes program

Our highly successful program for 3rd-6th grade girls is a perfect fit if you are new to the game, or if you want to continue learning about volleyball to get ready to join one of competitive teams in the future. This program is run by fantastic coaching staff! Our goal is to have players improve their technical skills regardless of their current level. Each session focuses on 4-5 practices (date-dependent) followed by a final competition day.

This clinic is historically where our current players have started their volleyball careers. We have seen players participate in the camp and then shine at tryouts and make teams or their school teams. Regardless of whether you choose to play for fun or to get ready to compete, this is a great way to get exercise, meet new friends, and play and learn a fun sport!

Indoor High Intensity

Get tryout-ready and have fun with engaging staff and fast-paced drills with our High Intensity Camp for incoming 7th-12th graders! This camp is carefully designed, featuring drills and training techniques that have propelled athletes to high-level college volleyball careers and beyond. Sessions feature training across all skills and positions. Camp sessions focus on high quality repetitions of each skill, and scrimmage opportunities are included in the majority of sessions. Get ready for club and school tryouts with this fantastic camp!
